    Qiskit Elements

    From fine-grained pulse control to high-level optimization algorithms, ranging through circuit optimization, noise simulation, hardware characterization, noise mitigation, and near-tem quantum computation, the Qiskit ecosystem covers all aspects of modern quantum research + development.

    • Terra

      Within Terra is a set of tools for composing quantum programs at the level of circuits and pulses, optimizing them for the constraints of a particular quantum device, and managing the batched execution of experiments on remote-access devices.

    • Aqua

      Qiskit Aqua contains a library of cross-domain quantum algorithms upon which applications for near-term quantum computing can be built. It currently allows the user to experiment on chemistry, AI, optimization and finance applications for near-term quantum computers.

    • Aer

      Qiskit Aer provides a high performance simulator framework. It contains optimized simulator backends for executing circuits compiled in Qiskit Terra, and tools for constructing highly configurable noise models enabling realistic noisy simulations of real devices.

    • Ignis

      Qiskit Ignis is a collection of experiments for understanding and mitigating noise in quantum circuits and systems. The experiments provided in Ignis are grouped into the topics of characterization, verification and mitigation.
